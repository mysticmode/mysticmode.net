Occurrence and non-occurrence
May 4, 2022

There is occurrence and non-occurrence. In-between we say there is life. This happens in the samsara, the world. Beyond that there is something omnipotent, let it be God or Nature. My point is that that is what I breath, we all breath and every living creatures breath as well.
